Baked Salmon With Lemon And Herbs

This recipe is a quick and flavorful way to prepare salmon, with the brightness of lemon and the earthiness of herbs. You can serve it with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables or quinoa.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 22 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 12 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • tbsp chopped fresh rosemary
  • 4 tbsp chopped fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  • Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Place salmon fillets on the baking sheet.
  • Drizzle olive oil over salmon, then top each fillet with a lemon slice, rosemary, and thyme.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Bake for 12 minutes or until salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  • Serve hot, garnished with additional herbs if desired.
